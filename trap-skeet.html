Hours of Operation

For up‑to‑date information check the “Range Schedule”

Note: All scheduled hours are weather permitting.


Summer Hours (April to October)

  • Saturday: 9am–2pm — Skeet & Trap Open (Last Squad 1:30pm)
  • Sunday: 9am–2pm — Skeet & Trap Open (Last Squad 1:30pm)
  • Thursday: Cancelled temporarily (Reopening when berm is completed)
  • Holidays: 9am–2pm — Skeet & Trap Open (Last Squad 1:30pm)
  • Last Sunday of the month — Range Closed

Winter Hours (November to March)

  • Saturday: 10:30am–2:30pm — Skeet & Trap Open (Last Squad 2:00pm)
  • Sunday: Not scheduled at this time
  • Thursday: Cancelled temporarily (Reopening when berm is completed)
  • Last Sunday of the month — Range Closed

Please arrive 30 minutes prior to Last Squad time to check available space.

The range closes after the last squad finishes shooting.

Extended hours available for groups, events, and private parties. Use the Contact Trap & Skeet card below.


Summer: May to end of October (approx.)

Summer season may extend if temperatures stay above freezing

Winter: Still in planning phase

2026 Member Pre‑Shot Packages

With the introduction of the Target Tag system we also have new package options for our members looking to pre‑purchase their rounds. The standard single round and 10 + 1 shooters card pricing is all still available.

The Bronze Package | $80

The Standard. Perfect for the casual weekend shooter who wants a simple, no‑fuss discount.

What you get: 11 Rounds of Trap or Skeet.

The Perk: Buy 10, get 1 FREE.

Savings: Covers your annual guest fee or a couple of boxes of shells.

The Silver Package | $150

The Monthly Regular. Designed for the shooter who outpaces the basic card but isn't at the club every single day.

What you get: 22 Rounds.

The Perk: Nearly 4 Rounds FREE.

Why it’s better: Drops your per‑round cost significantly below the standard $8 rate.

The Gold Package | $250

The Competitive Edge. For the league shooter or the family that shares a card.

What you get: 38 Rounds.

The Perk: Includes 7 FREE Rounds.

Why it’s better: You’re essentially shooting for $6.57 per round all season long.

The Platinum Package | $500

The Heavy Hitter. You’re here every week, shooting multiple rounds.

What you get: 80 Rounds.

The Perk: A massive 18 ROUNDS FREE.

Why it’s better: Locks in a 25% discount on your targets.

The Diamond Package | $1,000

The Ultimate Supporter. For our most dedicated members.

What you get: 165 Rounds.

The Perk: 40 ROUNDS FREE. (A $320.00 Added Value).

The Privilege: This brings your cost down to $6.06 per round and provides vital upfront capital for 2026.
