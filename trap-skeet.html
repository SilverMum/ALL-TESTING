Hours of Operation

For up‑to‑date information check the “Range Schedule”

Note: All scheduled hours are weather permitting.


Summer Hours (April to October)

  • Saturday: 9am–2pm — Skeet & Trap Open (Last Squad 1:30pm)
  • Sunday: 9am–2pm — Skeet & Trap Open (Last Squad 1:30pm)
  • Thursday: Cancelled temporarily (Reopening when berm is completed)
  • Holidays: 9am–2pm — Skeet & Trap Open (Last Squad 1:30pm)
  • Last Sunday of the month — Range Closed

Winter Hours (November to March)

  • Saturday: 10:30am–2:30pm — Skeet & Trap Open (Last Squad 2:00pm)
  • Sunday: Not scheduled at this time
  • Thursday: Cancelled temporarily (Reopening when berm is completed)
  • Last Sunday of the month — Range Closed

Please arrive 30 minutes prior to Last Squad time to check available space.

The range closes after the last squad finishes shooting.

Extended hours available for groups, events, and private parties. Use the Contact Trap & Skeet card below.


Summer: May to end of October (approx.)

Summer season may extend if temperatures stay above freezing

Winter: Still in planning phase
