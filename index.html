Membership Options

Join Our Community

Choose the membership that fits your lifestyle. OFHA membership available through
our club.

General Club

$150 /year

Full access to club facilities and social activities

  • All club facilities access
  • Voting rights at meetings
  • Discounted event fees
  • Euchre, darts, cornhole & horseshoes
  • Member newsletter
  • Community events

Trap & Skeet

$250 /year

Everything in General plus unlimited range access

  • All General Membership benefits
  • Unlimited trap & skeet access
  • Free clay targets (limit applies)
  • Competition discounts
  • Priority range booking
  • Equipment storage locker

OFHA

$45 /year

Provincial hunting & fishing advocacy membership

  • Ontario-wide advocacy
  • Conservation support
  • OFHA events access
  • Insurance benefits
  • Educational resources
  • Purchase through our club
